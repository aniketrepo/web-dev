Grilled Sandwiches

Grilled sandwiches are a quick and delicious snack made by layering bread with cheese, vegetables, or spreads, then grilling until golden, crispy, and perfectly melted inside. Theyâ€™re easy to prepare and customizable to suit any taste.

Ingredients

  • Bread slices (white, brown, or multigrain)
  • Butter or mayonnaise
  • Cheese slices or grated cheese
  • Vegetables (tomato, onion, cucumber, capsicum, etc.)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Optional: green chutney, mustard, or sauces

Steps

  1. Spread butter or mayo on one side of each bread slice.
  2. Add cheese, vegetables, and seasoning between two slices.
  3. Spread a little butter on the outside of the bread for crispiness.
  4. Grill in a sandwich maker, pan, or tawa until golden brown and the cheese melts.
  5. Serve hot with ketchup or chutney.
