Mac and Cheese

Mac and Cheese is a classic comfort food made with tender pasta coated in a rich, creamy cheese sauce. Itâ€™s simple to prepare, customizable with different cheeses or toppings, and perfect as both a main dish or a side for any meal.

Ingredients

  • Elbow macaroni (or any pasta)
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Cheddar cheese (or a mix of cheeses)
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Boil pasta until tender, then drain.
  2. In a pan, melt butter and whisk in flour to make a roux.
  3. Slowly add milk, stirring until smooth and thick.
  4. Add cheese, salt, and pepper to make a creamy sauce.
  5. Mix in the cooked pasta until fully coated.
  6. Optional: Bake with extra cheese on top for a golden crust.
